Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

TYM ("we," "us," or "our") provides an anonymous messaging experience that allows users to receive messages and media from others. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when you use TYM.

By using TYM, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: username, email address, encrypted credentials, and profile details such as avatar.
  • User Content: anonymous messages, uploaded images, and other content submitted through the app.
  • Technical Data: device/browser information, IP address, timestamps, and basic usage diagnostics for security and service reliability.
  • Authentication Data: login/session information handled via our authentication provider.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Deliver core app functionality (sending/receiving messages and media).
  • Protect users and the platform from abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.
  • Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve app performance and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Anonymous Messaging & Visibility

TYM is designed for anonymous messaging. Message recipients generally do not see identifying sender details by default. However, we may internally process technical metadata to maintain platform safety, prevent abuse, and comply with law.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use local storage, cookies, or similar technologies for session continuity, sign-in state, and user preferences. You can control certain browser storage settings, but disabling them may impact app functionality.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, storage, authentication, analytics) that support app operation.
  • To comply with applicable laws, legal process, or lawful governmental requests.
  • To protect rights, safety, and security of users, TYM, or others.
  • As part of a business transfer (e.g., merger, acquisition, or asset sale), where permitted by law.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary by data type and legal requirements.

8. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal data processing. You may also request account deletion where applicable. To make a request, contact us at the email below.

10. Children’s Privacy

TYM is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law in your jurisdiction. If you believe a child has provided personal information unlawfully, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers in accordance with applicable law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will revise the effective date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or privacy-related requests, contact us at privacy@tym.com.
